About Us

Vipanchika is the annual college fest of the School of Engineering, CUSAT, Kochi. Vipanchika blends a series of fine-arts, socio-cultural and literary events, along with a variety of exhibitions and informal events. Since its inception in 2002, Vipanchika never failed to amuse the viewers. The fest attracts thousands of visitors to the campus annually, and is one of the biggest cultural fests in Kerala.
